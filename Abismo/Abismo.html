ACCESS THE ABYSS

HISTORY

After a crash-landing on the seabed of an oceanic planet, a desperate crew of fugitives must recover the scattered components of their ship to repair their only way out of the abyss, only to uncover both the drowned remnants of an ancient civilization and the traces of a corporate expedition whose presence on the planet was anything but benign.

SURVIVE THE ABYSS

The seafloor changes every dive. Explore unstable caverns, derelict facilities, and bioluminescent ecosystems filled with predators, contamination hazards, collapsing structures, and failing equipment. Manage oxygen, light, sonar, and suit integrity, because...

the deeper you go, the fewer second chances you’ll get.

REPAIR YOUR WAY HOME

After crash-landing on the abyssal floor, retrieve the scattered components of your ship. These parts lie buried deep within derelict research facilities, collapsed trenches, and contaminated zones.

This is your only chance to escape.

ANALOG HORROR IN THE DARK

Piece together a branching, optional narrative through corrupted VHS logs, scrambled audio, and deteriorating terminals.

Follow the mystery to its end.

DIVE SOLO OR WITH FRIENDS

Abismo fully supports both singleplayer and co-op. Explore the abyss on your own with enhanced solo tools and pacing, or coordinate with up to four divers using proximity voice chat.
