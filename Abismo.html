ABISMO

ABISMO is a cooperative deep-sea horror game set on the abyssal seabed of an alien planet. Playable solo or with up to four players, the game blends tense survival, environmental exploration, and psychological horror, all wrapped in a retrofuturistic analog horror aesthetic.

After a catastrophic crash on the ocean floor, a stranded crew must descend into abandoned facilities and drowned ruins to recover the scattered pieces of their ship, their only chance of escaping the planet. The deeper they venture, the more the abyss reveals traces of past expeditions, corrupted technology, and entities shaped by forces humanity never understood.

ABISMO focuses on atmosphere over action, building dread through darkness, isolation, sound design, and the constant pressure of the deep.

CORE PILLARS

Co-op First, Solo Ready
Designed from the ground up for 1–4 players, with a fully supported solo experience.

Descent-Based Survival
A deep-sea dungeon crawler where every dive pushes you further into danger.

Oppressive Abyssal Atmosphere
Crushing darkness, isolation, and constant pressure beneath an alien ocean.

Analog Horror Tone
VHS aesthetics and distorted signals shape the game’s identity and narrative.

GAMEPLAY LOOP

  1. Prepare your dive and manage limited resources.
  2. Explore procedurally generated facilities and ruins.
  3. Recover ship components and valuable data.
  4. Survive environmental hazards and hostile entities.
  5. Return, if you can, and progress deeper next time.

Each dive pushes players further into the abyss, increasing both risk and narrative tension.

TARGET AUDIENCE

  • Fans of cooperative horror and survival experiences.
  • Players drawn to atmosphere-driven, slow-burn tension.
  • Indie horror enthusiasts interested in analog aesthetics.
  • Players who enjoy both replayable systems and narrative depth.
