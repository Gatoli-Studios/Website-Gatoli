PROJECT: ABISMO

ABISMO is a multiplayer cooperative horror roguelike set in the depths of an alien ocean world.

Players assume the role of four former maintenance workers on an interstellar oil platform who crash into the surface of a planet entirely covered by ocean.

Players must explore the oceanic environment, recover critical ship components scattered across the seabed, and survive against natural hazards and mysterious threats.
